Group creation helpdesk:
Welcome to the group creation menu. Here you can create the group of your dreams and set the settings to your liking.
Name:
Here you can choose any name that you would like your group to have
Categories:
Choose from multiple categories you want your group to be associated with.
To make more than one selection hold CTRL while making your selections.
Member limit:
Set the maximum amount of members for your group. Has to be a number.
Looking for members:
Mark the tickbox if you are looking for members and want your group to be visible in the groupfinder.
Add members:
Open a menu with a list of people you know that you can add to your group on creation or even after in the settings.
