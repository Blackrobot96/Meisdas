Finder helpdesk:
This is the screen you will encounter if there are no more groups to swipe through. Don't worry, there will surely be groups in the future that are looking for more members.
Managing groups:
In case there are no groups to join you will have the option of opening your group manager.
This can happen, because either, no groups are open right now or because you declined the current group requests.
In said group manager you can see your current groups and have the ability to create a new group.
